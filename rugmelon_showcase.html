About Us

RugMelon is a leading exporter from Panipat, India. With years of experience in the home textile industry, we focus on quality, design, and sustainability. Our products are crafted to meet international standards and cater to boutique brands, wholesalers, and importers worldwide.

Our vision is to bring Indian craftsmanship to global homes while maintaining eco-friendly production practices and supporting local artisans.
